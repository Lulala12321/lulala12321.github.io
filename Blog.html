Knowleage of art

Art has the ability to make our world more beautiful. Art also inspires people to think more and to dream more. Art is truly a blessing, and a very unique expression of human intelligence.

Colour Your World

1-5 November, 10am-5pm, Art work

Contemporary New Zealand artist Lisa Ferguson presents this exhibition of larger-than-life works that enhance environments by injecting a sense of life, energy and beauty.

Lisa creates large, vibrant works layered with stunning colours that have the power to invigorate the senses and lift spirits.

Full of energy and movement, they are compellingly beautiful and aim to draw viewers into their presence.

Also on display are limited edition cushions, plus a range of silk scarves that are stunning, walking works of art that have the power to colour your world, and your wardrobe.

Ticket Pricing: Free entry

Summer Sweetener Market

Sunday 3 Dec, 10am-4pm, Square of Art work

Kick off summer by satisfying all of your senses at the Arts Centre Summer Sweetener Market, featuring curated shopping, art, live music, food and drinks.

In response to the huge turnout to our last market, we are doubling the number of stalls and increasing the number of food trucks - and this time they will be located in the true home of Arts Centre markets - Square of Art work.

The markets will showcase Christchurch's finest independent designers, making it the perfect spot to find unique, local, handmade goods for your Christmas stash.

Ticket Pricing: Free entry
